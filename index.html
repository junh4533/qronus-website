The first self-manageable queuing system


Don't keep your clients waiting, keep them anticipating. Nobody wants to wait in line for an hour. With the Qronus Queue Management you can optimize appointment scheduling and manage them in one single system.

Fast and Inutuitive Check In


Unlike other queueing systems, the Qronus Queueing system supports magnetic card swiping for a faster check in.

Organized and Automated Queueing


Instead of using pen and paper to record the waitlist, our app automates the entire queueing process. When it is their turn, users are notified via the TV Display and a personalized text message.

Innovative Monitoring


Our app generates performance metrics like clients per hour, average queue time, and count per issue to optimize your queueing time. Our app also convieniently generates ITAM compatible support tickets to document the issue.
